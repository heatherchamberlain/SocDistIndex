Urban area extents were defined using two datasets GHS (Global Human Settlement) datasets: GHS Urban Centre Database 2015 v1.2 (Florczyk et al., 2019a) and GHS-SMOD v2.0 (Settlement Model grid; Pesaresi et al., 2019). The GHS Urban Centre Database (GHS-UCDB) consists of polygons representing urban centres based on population density and built-up area, with the degree of urbanisation methodology applied (Florczyk et al., 2019b).

- ucdb_pts_SSA51_KEN.shp is the subset of urban centres in Kenya 
- uext_polys_KEN.shp is the subset of the derived urban extent polygons for Kenya
